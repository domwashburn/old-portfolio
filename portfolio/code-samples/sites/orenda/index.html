What is Orenda Force?

The Orenda Force Team believes that every person deserves to perform, look, and feel the way they desire. Orenda offers the power for each individual to take charge of their lives and flourish. Our team wants to make everyone aware of this power and to encourage them to the action required to improve the quality of their health and well­ being.

Orenda Force is a wellness club that helps one achieve peak health, both of body and mind. We provide a service that assists and guides people in achieving their overall health goals.

Health of the body

Body health is attained through both physical training and nutritional plans customized to each individual.

Our Orenda Force facility is an equipped training center with the goal of improving balanced fitness among strength, endurance, and flexibility.

Personal nutrition planning is initiated through one-­on­-one consultation tailored to your individual goals and overall wellness.

Health of the mind

Health of the mind is improved through physical activity and sound nutrition.

To achieve peak well­ being of mind surpassing all standards, we offer Mind Performance Therapy.

Mind Performance Therapy is a drug­-free, natural procedure that corrects the brains processing routes. It is a therapeutic practice that results in optimized brain function for a plethora of benefits that results in one reaching their full potential.
